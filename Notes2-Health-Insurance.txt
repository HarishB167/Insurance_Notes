- Jhols
* (1) Sub Limits
> 50 lac cover, but can't take whole cover for a diseases
> Ideally policy shouldn't have any sub limit
* (2) Room rent cap
> One day room rent cannot be 1% of sum assured
(10,000 for 10 lac policy)
> If one takes 12000 rent room and pays 2000 from his pocket.
Then taking 83% claim. And only 83% claim will be given from overall
bill, i.e., In 7 lac claim only 5.8 lac will be given.
1.2 lac will be given from pocket.
> All majority expenses in hospital bill are linked with
room rent cap.
> Ideally policy shouldn't have any room rent cap
> Should have option have treatment in any type of room
* (3) Co-pay (12:00)
> Some policy has 20% or 30% co-pay.
>> Means for 10 lac bill - 3 lac by personal pocket and 7 lac by insurance company
>> There is no benefit of such a policy
> Co-pay reality
>> 20-30% is paid by pocket
>> Also from remaining companies part is removed - room rent, disease sublimit etc.
> In mentioned example for 15 lac policy, 10 lac was claimed and only 4 lac was given.
* (4) Waiting period
> 5 kinds
1. Standard waiting period (30 days)
> From buy date of policy.
> Accidents should be allowed.
2. Waiting period for critical illness (90 days)
> Ask from policy bazar representative about duration of waiting period
of critical illness. Also about list of ciritical illnesses included.
> Maternity claim waiting period (9-36 months)
>> Ask about this also.
3. Waiting period for pre-existing diseases (3-4 years)
> E.g. - diabetes, heart condition etc
> This is why take policy when your are healthy
4. Waiting period for slow growing diseases (2 years)
> Even if you don't have these disease when taking policy
> Treatments included
E.N.T. Disorders
Hernia
Cataract
Osteoprorsis
Joint Replacement etc.
5. Restoration of sum assured
> If you exhausted cover within a year, then there will be anxiety
for remaining months.
> This restoration feature will restore cover to original amount for the
remaining months.
> Conditions possible are
>> One-time restoration
>> Cannot use restoration for same disease
> Ask from policybazar executive about in which scenarios this feature 
will not be in use for me. What will it not cover?
6. Zonal vs Pan India policy
> There can be game in zonal policy
>> If address is of rural, then if treatment taken in urban, then there
can be 20% co-pay applied.
>> Should have taken city/urban policy
> Today new age policy are Pan india policies
7. Exclusions
> Should ask from policybazar executive about "Give me a list of Exclusions".
> Should know what treatments are not covered under policy.
> Example - In a policy there are 35 exclusions.
> Example - Any hospitalizations which are not Medically Necessary
>> Most of claims are rejected over this point.

- Biggest Jhol
* Some company are only allowing cashless claims.
* As if I got accident, then person can take me to any hospital
> And also if I want to do a plan surgery in a different better hospital
* POINT 1 : Should allow both cashless and reimbursement claims.
* POINT 2 : Should allow can go to any hospital, whether network hospital or other
hospital.
* Cashless claim is always better.
> As hospital will not give quality treatment.
> And in most cases hospital will deny the treatment till half money is
deposited by you.
* There is a war b/w insurance companies and hospitals
> Biggest loss is faced by patient, if we take cashless claims.
